Below is the biography of Prof Soyinka

  • 1934: Professor Soyinka was born
  • 1954: He attended Government College, Ibadan
  • 1954: He proceeded to University of Leeds
  • 1973: He obtained his doctorate degree from University of Leeds
  • 1960: He was awarded Rockefeller bursary and returned to Nigeria for further studies
  • 1960: He founded a theatre group called 'Mask'
  • 1964: He founded a theatre company called 'Orisun Theatre Company'
  • 1967: He was incarcerated for appealing in an article for cease fire during Biafra war
  • 1958-1959: His first book Swamp Dwellers and the Lion and the Jwell, written in London was performed at Ibadan.
  • 1974: His book, Jero's Metarmorphosis, written earlier was performed
  • 1977: His book, Opera Wonyosiwas performed
